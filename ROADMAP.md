# Habla 1.0 Roadmap

## Goal
Build a personal AI Spanish tutor that helps me become conversational through daily voice practice.

## Core Experience
Habla should feel like opening a private Spanish lesson with Carlos.

Carlos should:
- Talk with me naturally
- Correct my Spanish gently
- Remember what I struggle with
- Give me daily practice
- Help me prepare for real conversations
- Adapt to my level
