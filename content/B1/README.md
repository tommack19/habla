# B1 Intermediate

B1 teaches conversational independence.

Learners practice telling stories, giving opinions, describing past experiences, making plans, and handling conversation repair. Topics include family conversations, work stories, restaurant problems, travel experiences, health, invitations, and everyday opinions.

Grammar focuses on past tense contrast, future plans, reflexive verbs, comparatives, connectors, and more natural question follow-ups.

By the end of B1, learners should be able to hold longer conversations, explain needs, share experiences, and recover when they do not understand.
