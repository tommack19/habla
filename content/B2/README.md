# B2 Upper Intermediate

B2 teaches confident, flexible communication across familiar real-world topics.

Learners work with longer conversations, opinions, cultural topics, media, storytelling, problem solving, and nuanced family or social discussions. Lessons include listening to more natural Spanish, summarizing ideas, comparing viewpoints, and speaking with more flow.

Grammar focuses on advanced connectors, subjunctive foundations, conditional expressions, advanced past narration, and smoother transitions.

By the end of B2, learners should be able to participate in extended conversations, understand common native-speed context, and express detailed thoughts with increasing confidence.
